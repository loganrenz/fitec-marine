About FITEC

A Legacy of Marine Healthcare Innovation

Founded in 1987, FITEC (Functional Innovations in Therapeutic Cetacean Care) has been at the forefront of marine mammal healthcare for over three decades. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us the trusted partner of marine facilities worldwide.

Our team of dedicated scientists, veterinarians, and engineers work tirelessly to develop products that improve the health and wellbeing of cetaceans. From our state-of-the-art research facilities to our eco-friendly manufacturing processes, we ensure that every product meets the highest standards of quality and safety.

At FITEC, we believe that healthy oceans start with healthy marine life. That's why we're committed to sustainable practices and ongoing research to better understand and care for our ocean's gentle giants.
